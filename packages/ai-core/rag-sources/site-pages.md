## Operating pillars

Visitor-friendly overview of how CoCreate is organized:

- Brands / Strategy & Campaigns: Strategies and campaigns with measurable results.
- Digital Products: Web apps, intranets, and mobile experiences.
- Production / Studio: TV, digital video, and production craft.
- PR & Communications: Media relations, storytelling, and stakeholder messaging.
- Talent: Talent management, booking, and representation (including partner channels such as Zaralelle Ltd).
- Analytics & Insights: Data-driven strategy and measurement.
- Investor Relations: Financial communications and corporate investor reporting.
- XP / Experience: Brand activations, pop-ups, and large-scale event production.
- Exchange Content: Digital creation, video production, and broadcast/media execution.
